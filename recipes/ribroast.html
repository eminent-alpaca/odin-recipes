Rib Roast

Description

Beef, it's what's for dinner and this is a beaut

Ingredients:

  • 1 (5 pound) standing beef rib roast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Allow roast to stand at room temperature for at least 1 hour.

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Combine the salt, pepper and garlic powder in a small cup. Place the roast on a rack in a roasting pan so that the fatty side is up and the rib side is on the bottom. Rub the seasoning onto the roast.
  3. Roast for 1 hour in the preheated oven. Turn the oven off and leave the roast inside. Do not open the door. Leave it in there for 3 hours. (fr, don't do this. Danger Zone.)
  4. Turn the oven back on to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and reheat the roast for 30 to 40 minutes. The internal temperature should be at least 145 degrees F (62 degrees C). (fr, 130)
  5. Remove from the oven and let rest for 10 minutes before carving into servings.
